🚀 Project Overview

A smart AI-powered platform that helps users choose the best career path by simulating future outcomes using real-time market data, personal skills, and predictive analytics.

🎯 Problem Statement

Students and professionals struggle to choose the right career due to:

Lack of clarity about future opportunities
No real-time job market insights
No personalized guidance
Fear of making wrong decisions
💡 Solution

Build an AI Career Decision Simulator that:

Analyzes user profile
Matches careers
Simulates future outcomes
Provides actionable roadmap
